    About Me

    I'm Anshuman Bhardwaj, a creative tech enthusiast. My journey has been a fusion of coding, 3D modeling, animation, photography, and photo editing.


    Coding Aficionado

    The digital realm has always beckoned me. I've worn the hat of an IT team leader in school, contributing my tech-savvy skills to major functions and even shaping our school's online portal. Beyond the basics, I've ventured into the world of AI and machine learning. I once led a dynamic team to develop web and mobile applications aimed at assisting individuals with physical challenges. My code isn't just lines; it's a tool for positive change.


    3D Modeling and Animation

    The world of 3D modeling and animation captivates my creative spirit. I've delved into the magic of turning imagination into visual reality. From character modeling to intricate animations, I revel in the art of making the unreal feel lifelike.


    Photo Editing Whiz

    I wield my editing skills like a paintbrush on a canvas. Photo editing is my realm of artistic expression, where I transform ordinary images into stories that speak to the soul. Every edit is a journey to evoke emotions and engage the viewer.


    Photography Enthusiast

    When not behind a computer, I'm behind a camera. Photography is my passport to exploration. Whether capturing the vibrant hues of a bustling market or the tranquil beauty of nature, my lens tells stories in every click. Each photograph is a moment in time, a slice of life, and an opportunity to share the world as I see it.
